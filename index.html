It also has a class of ".bg-dark-trans" to turn the font color to a lighter shade. Other than that, the text you see is unadorned by any other styling classes.

This section is "space-around", which is like "space-tight" from above, but this one includes space between the elements. Refer to main css file to adjust whether you have "space-around" or "space-between".

"space-around" has half a space between the left of the container and the left most element (and half a space between the right of the container and the rightmost element). "space-between" puts the elements right up against the containing div.

These paragraphs have been constrained to have a maximum width rather than fill all the available space. (Otherwise they will grow to fill all the available space).

Click this paragraph to toggle between "space-between" and "space-around"
